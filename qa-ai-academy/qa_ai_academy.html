Welcome to QA-AI Academy

QA-AI Academy is a practical AI learning dashboard for software testers. You will practice prompting, assumption checking, risk triage, repeatable evaluation, evidence review, AI-assisted debugging, bounded accessibility testing, and reflective handoffs.

Start with Mission 01. This single file contains the complete campaign, embedded case files, and dependency-free lab harnesses. The first four missions build the judgment habits used in the later workbench and debugging missions.

Use ChatGPT or another AI assistant as a thinking partner: ask it to explain, draft, critique, and suggest checks. Do not let it replace your judgment.

Safety rule: use only synthetic, public, or explicitly approved non-sensitive data. Do not paste credentials, customer records, private code, or sensitive operational details into AI tools.

Evidence and verification matter because polished AI output can still be wrong. Your job is to trace claims back to current requirements, sources, files, tests, commands, browser behavior, or human review, then report what was not checked.
